About Coach Reza Abbasi

Coach Reza Abbasi is a 5th Dan Shotokan Karate instructor, an official Karate Federation referee, and a certified Level 1 Karate coach with over 15 years of experience in teaching and developing martial artists.

Throughout his career, Coach Abbasi has achieved multiple championship titles at national and international competitions, including a Gold Medal at the 2015 World Championship, and continues to share his knowledge, experience, and passion for professional Shotokan Karate with his students.

His mission is to teach karate as more than just a sport — it is a way to build confidence, discipline, respect, focus, and strong character. Through structured training, students learn valuable martial arts skills while developing both physically and mentally.

Coach Abbasi’s classes provide a safe, positive, and motivating environment where children, teens, and adults can improve their fitness, learn self-defense, develop focus, and work toward achieving their personal goals.

The goal is not only to teach karate techniques, but also to help students become confident, respectful, disciplined, and successful individuals both inside and outside the dojo.
